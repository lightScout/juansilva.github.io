// THE BRIEF

Puzzle game built entirely in Kotlin Multiplatform. First shipped product under Light Scout Ltd — embodying our values of learning through play, human-centred design, and calm, considered digital experiences. Game logic, state, persistence, and IAP backend all shared across platforms. Native UI on both — Compose for Android, SwiftUI integration for iOS.

Full-cycle ownership: design, development, QA, App Store + Play Store submissions, and ongoing maintenance across two independent app store ecosystems.

Kotlin Multiplatform throughout. Shared game engine with coroutines for frame updates. Compose Multiplatform for Android UI. SQLDelight for local persistence. In-app purchases via StoreKit2 (iOS) and Billing Library (Android).

Solo shipping experience — provisioning, TestFlight, App Store review cycles, Play Store release tracks, and version management across two independent ecosystems.

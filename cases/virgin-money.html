// THE BRIEF

Virgin Money was in the midst of a Java-to-Kotlin migration. I contributed to the modernization effort while building features that customers used daily — accounts, payments, card management, and support chat.

Worked across the legacy Java codebase and emerging Kotlin modules, learning constraint resolution under real production pressure.

Mixed Java and Kotlin codebase during transition. Traditional Android architecture (Activities, Fragments) being refactored toward MVVM. Manual DI initially, evolving toward Hilt.

Exposure to banking domain constraints — PCI compliance, transaction atomicity, regulatory reporting — shaped my understanding of why certain patterns exist in fintech.

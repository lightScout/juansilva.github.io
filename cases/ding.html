// THE BRIEF

Ding is a mobile engagement platform connecting businesses with customers. My first role involved building features across the Android app—UI, data sync, offline handling, and the unglamorous but essential work of keeping an app stable under real-world constraints.

No frameworks, no patterns yet—just core Android APIs, SQLite, and learning through shipping.

Pure Java. Activities, Fragments, Services, and ContentProviders. SQLite for persistence, SharedPreferences for configuration, REST APIs via Retrofit. Manual HTTP handling for edge cases.

Battery and bandwidth optimization under real conditions—mobile networks are unreliable, devices are heterogeneous, users expect things to work anyway.

// THE BRIEF

Android mobile app for Ding — the world's leading global mobile top-up service. Ding helps people send little bytes of happiness: prepaid credit to mobile phones across 150+ countries and 700+ operators. First professional mobile engineering role. Built features enabling secure, fast, reliable top-up purchasing directly on Android devices, integrating with payment partners, operator APIs, and retail networks spanning 600k+ outlets globally.

Shipped updates across the growth phase — from MVP to market-leading scale. 500 million top-ups sent. Focus on user experience, payment security, operator integration, and platform stability serving users in emerging markets and developed economies alike.

Android native development. Java codebase with RESTful integration to payment gateways and operator backends. Secure credential handling, transaction logging, and fraud detection. Real-time operator feeds for country and operator selection. In-app purchase flow, retail partner integrations, push notifications.

Collaborated with backend teams, operator partners, and compliance to navigate international payments, currency handling, KYC/AML requirements, and country-specific regulations. Early exposure to fintech infrastructure at scale.

Secure integration with payment gateways, credit/debit processing, and local payment methods. PCI compliance, encryption, and fraud detection across markets.

Operator API Integration

Real-time feeds from 700+ mobile operators across 150+ countries. Dynamic pricing, availability, and redemption status—ensuring fresh data for every transaction.

Retail partner APIs connecting 600k+ outlets globally. In-store purchasing, partner dashboards, inventory management, and commission workflows.

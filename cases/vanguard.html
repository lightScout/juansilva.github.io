// THE BRIEF

Vanguard UK needed a mobile-first investment experience. I focused on authentication systems, app architecture resilience, and user experience optimization for mission-critical financial transactions.

Owned design systems integration, securities trading UX, and performance optimization for high-frequency trading scenarios.

Kotlin throughout. Auth0 integration for secure authentication, Jetpack Compose for adaptive UI, MVVM with Clean Architecture, and Hilt for scalable dependency injection.

Comprehensive test coverage (JUnit, Mockito, Espresso) with a focus on reliability — downtime is not an option in fintech.

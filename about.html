// ABOUT

Started shipping Android in 2015 at Ding (Dublin). Moved through Athlon (fleet management, Amsterdam), Wipro (consumer apps, MVVM migration), Virgin Money (fintech, Java→Kotlin), HSBC First Direct (transaction enrichment, auth redesign), UsTwo × FT (KMP, modern architecture), and Vanguard UK (authentication platform at scale).

Mid-Vanguard, founded Light Scout Ltd — a London-based studio building human-centred software. We combine design, technology, and care to deliver calm, considered digital experiences with focus on data privacy, safety, and learning through play. Now leading Android at Sitka while growing Light Scout as a vehicle for building and shipping products guided by curiosity, clarity, and the belief that technology should expand human capability.

Kotlin-first, architecture-driven, test-obsessed. CLEAN architecture, MVVM, Hilt, Jetpack Compose. KMP exploration from Oct 2022 (personal projects including unshipped Mozzy — an AI privacy browser). First professional KMP at UsTwo × FT (Oct 2023). Now shipping production KMP via Light Scout Ltd — Cityscape City Puzzles (2026), live on App Store and Play Store.

Strong in CI/CD, release engineering, performance optimization. Fintech background shaped my discipline around compliance, security, and transactional consistency. I believe in clean separation of concerns, comprehensive testing (unit, integration, UI), and shipping things that scale. No heroes. No cruft.

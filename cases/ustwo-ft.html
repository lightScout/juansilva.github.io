// THE BRIEF

Financial Times needed cross-platform analytics — one source of truth for Android, iOS, and Web. Solution: Kotlin Multiplatform library sharing business logic and event tracking. First professional KMP engagement — proof the paradigm worked in production.

Simultaneously refactored Android architecture and optimized CI/CD pipelines to reduce feedback loops for the 50+ person mobile team.

Kotlin Multiplatform throughout. Shared business logic, event types, and validation. Ktor Client for HTTP. Android UI via Jetpack Compose and CLEAN architecture, Dagger2 for DI.

Shipped a developer experience focused library — eliminated 80% of analytics boilerplate, unified event tracking across platforms, and established patterns the FT team continues to use.
